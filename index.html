About me

I am an Honors Dual Degree Mechanical Engineering student studying at the Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, NY, class of 2018. In order to broaden my technical background I am also minoring in Electrical Engineering. Outside of classes I am heavily involved in the Aero Design Club as well as the school's American Society of Mechanical Engineers chapter, holding e-board positions within both organizations.

I have a passion for the aerospace/aviation industry and thus am striving to begin my career there. Ultimately I hope to hold a position leading design teams for military and defense contracts or space exploration programs.

I have a hobby-level interest in RC Aircraft, including both fixed wing and multi-rotor types. I also enjoy building and modifying personal computers.
