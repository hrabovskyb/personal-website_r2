Recycling

Recycling is an important component of protecting the environment and helping our local communities. It helps conserve resources and energy, preserves valuable landfill space and supports a healthy environment.

Facts:

  • Recycling helps to conserve our natural resources, such as coal, oil, and gas.

  • Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for three hours.

  • Recycling a single days worth of the Sunday New York Times saves 75,000 trees.

  • Plastic bags that are thrown into the ocean kill over a million sea creatures a year.

Tips:

  • Learn what can and can't be recycled in your area.

    • Each city has its own specifics, so try to follow the guidlines in your area.

    • Contact your waste removal company for more information.

  • Buy recycled products and be sure to continue to recycle them!

    • If you cannot buy recycled products, be sure to look for recyclable items.

  • Donate or sell old/broken electronics and appliances rather than disposing of them.
